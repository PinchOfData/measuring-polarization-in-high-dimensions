PyTorch implementation of the penalized multinomial partisanship estimator
of Gentzkow, Shapiro, and Taddy (2019, Econometrica). The multinomial
inverse regression framework is due to Taddy (2013, 2015); the penalized
estimator is due to Taddy (2017) and Gentzkow, Shapiro, and Taddy (2019).
The package also supports subsampling confidence intervals and unseen-text
scaling. For applications, see Cagé, Le Pennec, and Mougin (2024),
Osnabrügge, Ash, and Morelli (2023), and Widmer, Abed Meraim, Galletta,
and Ash (2022).
